# Forensic Audio Origin Boundary

The forensic audio suite has a serious personal origin and should be handled with care.

## Private Origin Boundary

The project originated during a traumatic personal period involving audio, severe distress, and later reflection about harm, safety, and responsibility.

The raw private details should not be included in buyer-facing material.

## Buyer-Safe Frame

The forensic audio work should be framed as a lawful evidence-integrity and chain-of-custody platform.

Buyer-safe themes:

- consent-aware evidence workflows
- chain-of-custody preservation
- tamper-evident logs
- forensic review discipline
- privacy boundaries
- audit trails
- conversion of a dangerous personal lesson into harm-prevention tooling

## Personal Safety Boundary

The inventor should not be required to personally process or listen to audio evidence as part of productization, diligence, or demos.

Future work should support:

- transcript-first review
- visual waveform/spectrogram summaries
- automated metadata extraction
- redacted demo files
- delegated or third-party audio handling
- opt-out controls for triggering media

## HPP / BACL Connection

This origin strengthens the need for:

- BACL-style integrity protections
- forensic chain-of-custody manifests
- strict access logs
- private data separation
- consent and authorization records
- deterministic safety gates around sensitive workflows

## Claim Boundary

Do not present the forensic suite as surveillance tooling.

Position it as evidence integrity, authorized review, and harm-prevention infrastructure.
