# Vision

HYPERPLASTICITY PROTOCOL V5 is a system for intentional self-directed change.

It is not a habit tracker, productivity board, therapy app, or journal by itself. It borrows from all of those, but its center is different: it helps the user observe state, choose a protocol, execute a loop, reflect on the result, and gradually discover what actually changes them.

## Design Feel

- Calm, precise, and capable.
- More cockpit than dashboard.
- More training room than social feed.
- More lab notebook than motivational poster.
- Beautiful, but never decorative at the expense of usefulness.

## Product Pillars

### 1. State

The app begins with where the user is right now.

Track signals like energy, focus, mood, stress, sleep, clarity, body tension, confidence, and desire to avoid.

### 2. Protocols

Protocols are repeatable training loops.

They should have an intent, trigger, duration, steps, expected effect, and reflection prompt.

### 3. Identity

The app should help the user name who they are becoming.

Identity should be practical, not fluffy. A chosen identity changes the recommended protocols, daily prompts, and review lens.

### 4. Evidence

The system should show what actually works.

Every protocol run can produce evidence: before state, after state, notes, resistance, outcome, and follow-up.

### 5. Companion Intelligence

AI should behave like a thoughtful training partner.

It should summarize patterns, ask sharp questions, suggest experiments, and help convert messy experience into clear next moves.

## Original Hypothesis

V5 preserves the original HPP hypothesis:

- Plasticity is trained through repeated loops.
- Safe repetition stabilizes useful patterns.
- The Habit-14 threshold marks the beginning of stabilization.
- Stabilized patterns become faster, more protected pathways.
- Stress and nurture require different system behavior.
- Evidence matters more than motivational intensity.

In V5, the human is the adaptive system. The app becomes the scaffold around that adaptation.

## North Star Experience

The user opens HPP and immediately knows:

- What state they are in.
- What loop they are training.
- What the next useful action is.
- What has been working lately.
